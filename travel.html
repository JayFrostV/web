Inside Vietnam luxury hotel rated among world's best

By Anh Minh May 5, 2024 | 12:00 pm GMT+7

Have a look at Banyan Tree Lang Co, the only representative in Vietnam named on Tripadvisor’s 2024 list of 25 best luxury hotels in the world.

On April 24, the American online travel agency ranked Banyan Tree Lang Co 12th on its list of 25 greatest luxury resorts. Situated between the Truong Son Mountains and the sea, the hotel has 86 villas with private pools.

Mountain villas cost VND15-25 million ($591-986) per night, depending on the number of bedrooms.

The furniture is designed in the deep wood tones of Vietnamese villages, combined with modern architecture to maximize natural light and sea views. The image above is a 260 meter-square villa with a living room and 3 bedrooms (the most luxurious option). The living room opens to the dining room, kitchen and mini bar that is appropriated for pool cocktail parties.

The master bedroom with an ocean view is a place to see the sunrise over Chan May Harbor and the Truong Son mountains.

The bathrooms include large bathtubs, large sinks, large mirrors and other toiletries.

Infinity pools at every villa are one of the resort’s most popular attractions, especially at Oceanview Pool Villas near the cliff. “About 860-meters above the water is a wonderful way to admire the stunning beauty of Lang Co Bay,” a customer commented. Beach Pool Villas cost from VND11.5 million ($453), while Lagoon Pool Villas start at VND8.5 million ($335).

Located “in the middle of nowhere,” Banyan Tree boasts peace and relaxation. Guests are encouraged to go stand up paddle boarding (SUP), play a round of golf and explore the local paddy fields.

The Banyan Tree Spa is also a must-try.

Banyan Tree also keeps guests happy with its three main restaurants. The Water Court restaurant satisfies diner with its take on the specialties of all three regions of Vietnam. The Azura serves Mediterranean cuisine, while Saffron attracts Thai food lovers. The eatery has been praised for its traditional fare and Thai original favorites.

Romantic Destination Dining near the beach in Lang Co bay is another lavish experience.

Families with young customers are fond of bird watching activities at Banyan Tree as well as free sight-seeing along a nearly kilometer-long river than runs through the resort. Only a 60-minute drive from the Da Nang and Phu Bai (Hue) airports, Banyan Tree in Lang Co is a destination for a luxurious trip.
